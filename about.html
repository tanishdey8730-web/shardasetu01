About Sharda Setu

Sharda Setu is a free learning hub for competitive exam aspirants and engineering students. We bring together curated YouTube lessons, placement prep tracks, career roadmaps, and interactive quizzes — designed to work well even on slower internet connections.

What Sharda Setu Does Today

Sharda Setu (Sanskrit: Sharda = knowledge, Setu = bridge) is a bridge between learners and quality study material. Instead of searching dozens of channels, you get one organized place for exam preparation and tech careers.


Online Education has three learning tracks:

  • Government Exams — SSC, CDS, AFCAT, NDA, RRB and more, with Maths, Physics & Chemistry playlists in Hindi and English.
  • IT & Placement Prep — DSA, Python, Full Stack, Aptitude, HR interviews, TCS NQT, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, and Java for placements.
  • Roadmaps — YouTube video roadmaps plus PDF and interactive links (roadmap.sh, Whimsical) for Python, Java, DSA, AI/ML, Data Science, DevOps, AWS, and Full Stack.

Quiz lets you test yourself with 10-question quizzes. Government subjects include Maths, Physics, Chemistry, and General Studies & Current Affairs. IT subjects include Aptitude, DSA, Python, Full Stack, HR Interview, and TCS NQT. You earn Gold, Silver, or Bronze medals based on your score.


Create a free profile to save your target exam (SSC CGL, TCS NQT, DSA, etc.) and jump straight to the right course track. The site runs on a lightweight Node.js backend and also works with built-in offline fallbacks when you open pages directly.
